Assessment & Grading Framework

Rating Level Description
★★★ Advanced Consistently demonstrates the skill independently with confidence, creativity, and beyond age-level expectations.
★★ Age Appropriate Demonstrates understanding of the skill and applies it with confidence appropriate to their developmental stage.
★ Getting There Requires support and encouragement to understand and apply the skill effectively; still developing at their own pace.

Evaluation Levels — Observation Key

Level Meaning
A Demonstrates clear understanding of the skill and applies it independently with confidence.
B Demonstrates understanding of the skill but requires time and guidance for consistent performance.
C Requires support to understand and apply the skill effectively.
